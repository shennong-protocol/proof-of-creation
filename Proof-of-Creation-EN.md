## The Problem We're Solving

You know the feeling.

You've watched the hashrate charts at 3 AM. You've calculated your electricity costs down to the decimal. You've seen good miners go bankrupt after a halving. You've felt the isolation—either the loneliness of holding while everyone else sells, or the anxiety of watching your investment bleed.

**2024 was brutal.** The halving hit hard. 15-20% of miners went underwater. The big players pivoted to AI. The small ones? They're still looking for a way out.

And here's the thing nobody talks about: **your health.**

You're burning out. You're stressed. You're sitting too long, sleeping too little, eating whatever's convenient. You've optimized your mining operation to perfection—but what about your body? Your mind? Your actual life?

We built Bitcoin to escape centralized control. But in the process, did we lose control of something else?

We believe **the same logic can extend beyond money.**

Your body is a node. Your health is the network. Your daily choices are your work.

## What Does This Mean in Practice?

### 10% Lifetime Share — The Founder's Reward

In Bitcoin, Satoshi still has millions of coins. They created the system; they benefit from it.

In Proof of Creation, **any project initiator gets 10%—forever.** You start something, you benefit from it. No hidden equity. No dilution. Just code saying: "You started this. You own this."

*10%永享 = 发起人激励 = 长期主义的保证*

### 70% to Contributors — The Miners' Cut

In Bitcoin, miners do the work and get the bulk of the reward.

In Proof of Creation, **70% goes to whoever does the work.** Whether it's cooking, coding, creating, or caring—those who contribute get the lion's share.

*70%给执行人 = 矿工经济 = 干活的人拿大头*

### 20% Public Welfare Fund — The Network Fee That Protects You

In Bitcoin, miners pay a small fee to keep the network alive.

In Proof of Creation, **20% goes to a welfare fund that protects every member.**

This isn't charity. **This is insurance.**

When you're young and healthy, you're contributing. When you're old or sick or struggling, the fund is there. You're paying for your future self. You're creating a network where **no one falls through the cracks.**

*20%公益基金 = 互助保险 = 既利他也自保*

## The Three-Phase Evolution

Just like Bitcoin didn't become Bitcoin in a day, **Proof of Creation grows with you:**

### Phase 1: Founding (Now)
- Core team builds infrastructure
- Early community members shape governance
- Systems are tested and refined

### Phase 2: Transition (3-5 Years)
- Leadership steps back
- Community takes governance
- Automated systems handle distribution

### Phase 3: Autonomy (5+ Years)

- Self-sustaining
- The community owns itself
